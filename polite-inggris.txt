POLITE REQUEST

# Formal Polite Request - “could” dan “would”
## Asking for permission (Meminta izin)
Rumus: Can/Could/May/Might + I + bare infinitive + Complement (optional) + ?
Contoh: Could I borrow your camera? (Bolehkah aku meminjam kameramu?)

## Asking for help (Meminta bantuan)
Rumus:Can/Could/Will/Would + You (please) + bare infinitive + Complement (optional) + ?
ATAU
Would + You mind + Gerund + Complement (optional) + ?
Contoh: Would you mind returning this to Mr.Padolski? (Maukah kamu mengembalikan ini kepada Mr.Padolski?)

# Informal Polite Request - “can” dan “will”
Can I borrow your pencil? (Bolehkah saya meminjam pensilmu?)
Will you give me your ticket to concert tonight? (Maukah kamu memberikanku tiketmu untuk konser mala mini?)

# Positive Answers To Request
Yes, Sure, Okay, Of course, My pleasure, No problem, Certainly dan masih banyak yang lainnya.

# Negative Answers To Request
Sorry, No, I’m sorry I can’t, Not now
